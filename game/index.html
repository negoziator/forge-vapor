Forge & Vapor

Welcome to a Laravel‑inspired mini‑game! Your mission is to help Vapor (Laravel’s serverless platform) collect Git stars that fly out of Forge (the deployment tool). Each glowing bar represents a star for your repository. Use the arrow keys (or A/D) to move left and right. Rack up as many stars as you can before the timer runs out, and try not to drop them—each miss counts as an error!
